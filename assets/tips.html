Tip 3: 9 Easy yet impactful ways to support locals when you travel

One of the most important lessons I have learned in my eight years of traveling, is to find impactful ways to support locals when you travel!
You may not realize it, but most of the time when you book a generic hotel, or buy a souvenir, the money does not go to the locals! Usually it is a foreign company and most typical souvenirs come from China! It may take a little extra effort to find them, but here are some easy ways you can support the locals instead!

1 Stay at BnB’s, Homestays or Guesthouses
2 Eat at locally owned restaurants
3 Buy local handicrafts and art
4 Shop at local farmer’s markets and grocers
5 Participate in a local experience
6 Donate clothes
7 Volunteer or clean up!
8 Talk to the locals!
9 Spread the word about them and their country!
